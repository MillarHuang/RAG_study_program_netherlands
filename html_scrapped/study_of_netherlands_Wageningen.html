Type of institution

The Netherlands has three main types of institutions:
  • Universities of applied sciences offer programmes that focus on the application of arts and sciences.
  • Research universities educate students in academic study and research.
  • Institutes for international education offer advanced training and courses in a wide range of fields.
Other institutions may also offer international degree programmes or short courses.
